What we are watching

Data centers, transmission lines, energy transitions, and large infrastructure builds are running into an organized opposition wave that did not exist five years ago. Townships are mobilizing earlier. Local journalism is sharper. One viral post can put a half-billion-dollar project on hold. The companies that come through the next two years are the ones whose communications leadership saw the pattern early and had a plan that started before the first public hearing, not after the local council vote.

Frequently Asked

Questions before you pick up the phone.

What is your response time?

Initial private conversation typically within 24 hours. War room standup within 72 hours when the situation requires it.

Do you work alongside in-house counsel?

Yes. Most engagements are co-led with General Counsel. Evandr handles communications strategy and reputation; counsel handles legal exposure. The work is sequenced together.

What size client do you typically work with?

Engagements typically range from $30,000 to mid six figures, depending on scope and duration. Smaller engagements are considered case by case.

Are you a registered lobbyist?

No. Evandr provides communications and reputation strategy. We coordinate with registered government relations counsel when the engagement requires it.

Where are you based?

Tampa headquarters, with team in Los Angeles, New York, and Tampa.

How does confidentiality work?

Engagements are private by default. Client names, project details, and outcomes are not discussed externally without explicit authorization.
